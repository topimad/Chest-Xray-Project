Welcome To Chest X-ray


Chest X-ray Platform harnesses the power of artificial intelligence models to revolutionize the classification of chest X-ray images. By employing advanced algorithms, this platform assists medical professionals in swiftly determining whether an X-ray depicts a normal condition, COVID-19 infection, or pneumonia. Through rapid and accurate analysis, it equips doctors with vital insights, enabling them to make informed decisions promptly. Moreover, the platform goes beyond mere classification by generating comprehensive medical reports, further streamlining the diagnostic process and enhancing patient care.
